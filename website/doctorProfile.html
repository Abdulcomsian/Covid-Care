    • About

    Islamabad Diagnostic Centre (IDC) is the only Diagnostic Centre, the main tertiary is in Islamabad while spreading all over Pakistan providing the entire spectrum of advanced diagnostics services in radiology and pathology under one roof on a 24/7 basis, 365 days a year. Since its inception, IDC has built a successful track record and is known for its latest imaging technologies, the broad range of laboratory tests, and quick turn-around times, with over 450 staff members and one of Pakistan’s largest pool of qualified pathologists and radiologists on its team.

    Islamabad Diagnostic Centre (IDC) has received the Best Enterprise Award from the European Business Assembly in 2015 in Oxford, UK.
