    • About

    Dr. Zainab Maqsood is a very well regarded Gynecologist and has a number of certifications under her
    belt, including M.B.B.S, F.C.P.S. (Gynecology & Obstetrician) and M.R.C.O.G-I (UK) as well as 6 years of experience in her field. Dr. Zainab Maqsood is capable of providing a diverse array of treatments and services including Antenatal Care, Antenatal Checkup, Caesarean (C-Section), Contraceptive Advice, Endoscopic Surgery, gynaecological complications like fibroid and ovarian cyst, Gynaecological Surgeries, heavy menstrual bleeding, Hysterectomy, Infertility Treatment, intrauterine growth retardation and Preterm Labour. She is also very punctual, so her patients only have to wait Under 15 Min for their consultations. These patients were particularly impressed by the doctor checkup, staffbehaviour and clinic environment.
